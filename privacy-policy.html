Legal & Compliance Information

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11th June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Millard Agingu & Company Advocates collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information in connection with our website and legal services.

Our Privacy Principles

  • We collect only necessary personal data.
  • We retain data only as long as required.
  • We prioritise confidentiality and legal privilege.
  • We are transparent about how data is used.

1. Who We Are

We are a law firm based in Nairobi, Kenya providing legal services to local and international clients. This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data processed in connection with our website, legal services, consultations, communications, and professional engagements.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Enquiry messages and communications
  • Payment or transaction-related details
  • Job application data (CVs, references, etc.)

Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Website usage data
  • Analytics data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing legal services
  • Responding to enquiries
  • Client onboarding and due diligence
  • Legal documentation and transactions
  • Billing and compliance obligations
  • Website and service improvement
  • Fraud prevention and security

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the Data Protection Act, 2019 (Kenya) and where applicable the GDPR.

  • Consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legal obligation
  • Legitimate interests
  • Vital interests
  • Public interest / legal claims

5. How We Share Information

  • Service providers (IT, cloud, communications, hosting)
  • Professional advisors (auditors, insurers, consultants)
  • Courts, regulators, and authorities where required
  • Counterparties in legal matters
  • Internal firm operations
  • Business restructuring or transfer scenarios

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including access controls, secure systems, confidentiality obligations, and monitoring procedures.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside Kenya. Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable law including the Data Protection Act, 2019 and GDPR.

8. Your Rights

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction
  • Right to object
  • Right to data portability (GDPR)
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to complain to regulators

9. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools (including Google Analytics) to improve website performance, understand usage patterns, and enhance user experience.
