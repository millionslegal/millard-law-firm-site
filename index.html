About the Firm

A Modern Boutique Legal Practice for a Global Clientele

Millard Agingu & Company Advocates is a boutique legal practice based in Kenya, focused on delivering sophisticated, commercially driven legal solutions to businesses, investors, and international clients.

The firm has a particular focus on cross-border legal advisory, data protection compliance, immigration and investment structuring, and property transactions, with a growing specialization in providing legal services to Spanish-speaking clients entering or operating in Kenya.

We operate at the intersection of law, business, and international mobility, offering tailored legal guidance designed for clients who require clarity, discretion, and strategic thinking.
