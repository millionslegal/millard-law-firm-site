Immigration Framework in Kenya

Immigration into Kenya is regulated by the Kenya Citizenship and Immigration Act. Foreign nationals intending to work, invest, or reside in Kenya are required to obtain the appropriate permits and approvals.

Work Permits and Investor Permits

Kenya offers various classes of work permits depending on employment type, investment activity, and professional engagement.

  • Work permits for employees and professionals
  • Investor permits for business owners and shareholders
  • Special passes for short-term assignments
  • Dependent and spouse permits

Common Challenges for Foreign Applicants

  • Incomplete documentation or incorrect filings
  • Delays in government processing timelines
  • Misclassification of permit categories
  • Unclear investment structuring for eligibility
